Organizations that helps with Human trafficking

Alliance to End Slavery and Trafficking (ATEST) :
Their mission is “It strives to prevent and end all forms of human trafficking and modern slavery around the world.”
Website : http://www.endslaverynow.org/alliance-to-end-slavery-and-trafficking-atest

Child Labor Coalition:
Their mission is "The Child Labor Coalition (CLC) exists to serve as a national network for the exchange of information about child labor; provide a forum and a unified voice on protecting working minors and ending child labor exploitation; and develop informational and educational outreach to the public and private sectors to combat child labor abuses and promote progressive initiatives and legislation.”
Website : http://www.endslaverynow.org/child-labor-coalition

Cotton Campaign:
Their mission is “The Cotton Campaign is a coalition of human rights organizations, trade unions, socially responsible investors and business associations united to end forced labor of children and adults in the cotton industry in Uzbekistan.”
Website : http://www.endslaverynow.org/cotton-campaign

Covenant House Washington D.C:
Their mission is “The Covenant House Mission statement helps us stay true to our calling as we serve homeless youth: We who recognize God's providence and fidelity to His people are dedicated to living out His covenant among ourselves and those children we serve, with absolute respect and unconditional love. That commitment calls us to serve suffering children of the street, and to protect and safeguard all children. Just as Christ in His humanity is the visible sign of God's presence among His people, so our efforts together in the covenant community are a visible sign that effects the presence of God, working through the Holy Spirit among ourselves and our youth.”
website: http://www.endslaverynow.org/covenant-house-washington-dc

FAIR Girls:
Their mission is : “FAIR Girls (formerly FAIR Fund) prevents the exploitation of girls worldwide with empowerment and education. Through prevention education, compassionate care, and survivor inclusive advocacy, FAIR Girls creates opportunities for girls to become confident, happy, healthy young women.”
Website : http://www.endslaverynow.org/fair-girls

For more organizations go to: http://www.endslaverynow.org/connect?country=1405&state=3896&city=3897#filter
