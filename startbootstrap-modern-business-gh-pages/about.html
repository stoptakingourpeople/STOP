What is STOP


STOP stands for "stop taking our people". The website provides general infromation and statistics about human trafficking. The site also presents diffrent stories of human trafficking. Our Stories page includes documentaries, articles and TED talks. In addition, our assistance page has other links to organizations that advocate against human trafficking. The assistance page also has self defence classes.

Why?


STOP is a website created to inform and educate people about the issue of human trafficking, especially in the District of Columbia. The problem of human trafficking is often underestimated. Many people, especially minorities girls have disappeared from our community. By bringing awareness, we hope that people learn the steps they can take to stop human trafficking. In addition, our website provides sources for victims of human trafficking.

How?


We learned how to make this website and many more computer science skills due to Girls Who Code. GWC had a summer immmersion program we atteneded. At the end of the program we had to do a project based on our intrest For our project, we decided to create this website.
