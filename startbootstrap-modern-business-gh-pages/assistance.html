Protect Yourself:


Make sure someone knows of your location whenever you're alone

Stay aware of your surroundings

When listening to music make sure it's quiet enough that you are still able to hear everything that is happening around you

No matter where you are make sure that your location is on

Protect Others:


If you know something, SAY SOMETHING. If you have any reason to believe that someone you know might be in danger, don't hesitate to call 911 or the human trafficking hotline.

If someone you know is tay on th phone with them if they are walking alone at night and even in the day time

Organizations Fighting Human Trafficking

Alliance to End Slavery and Trafficking (ATEST) :
Mission: “To prevent and end all forms of human trafficking and modern slavery around the world.”
Website : http://www.endslaverynow.org/alliance-to-end-slavery-and-trafficking-atest

Child Labor Coalition:
Mission: "To serve as a national network for the exchange of information about child labor; provide a forum and a unified voice on protecting working minors and ending child labor exploitation; and develop informational and educational outreach to the public and private sectors to combat child labor abuses and promote progressive initiatives and legislation.”
Website : http://www.endslaverynow.org/child-labor-coalition

Cotton Campaign:
Mission: “To end forced labor of children and adults in the cotton industry in Uzbekistan.”
Website : http://www.endslaverynow.org/cotton-campaign

Covenant House Washington D.C:
Mission: “The Covenant House Mission statement helps us stay true to our calling as we serve homeless youth: We who recognize God's providence and fidelity to His people are dedicated to living out His covenant among ourselves and those children we serve, with absolute respect and unconditional love. That commitment calls us to serve suffering children of the street, and to protect and safeguard all children. Just as Christ in His humanity is the visible sign of God's presence among His people, so our efforts together in the covenant community are a visible sign that effects the presence of God, working through the Holy Spirit among ourselves and our youth.”
Website: http://www.endslaverynow.org/covenant-house-washington-dc

FAIR Girls:
Mission: “[To prevent] the exploitation of girls worldwide with empowerment and education. Through prevention education, compassionate care, and survivor inclusive advocacy, FAIR Girls creates opportunities for girls to become confident, happy, healthy young women.”
Website : http://www.endslaverynow.org/fair-girls
