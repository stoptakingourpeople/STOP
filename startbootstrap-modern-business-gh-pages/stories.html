Missing Persons

Of the original 10 missing girls in the D.C. area, several of them were found, but their still is a large amount of unsolved cases in the Metropolitan area when the timeline is increased. Here are some of the victims’ profiles.These cases could possibly be related with human trafficking

Relisha Tenau Rudd

Relisha Tenau Rudd was an 8 year old girl lived in a homeless shelter with her mother.

It was suspected to have been abducted by Khalil Tatum, the janitor of the shelter, who was recently found March 2014 in the Washington, D.C. park. He was discovered with tools that looked as if they were to bury a child’s body which is why Relisha has been presumed dead.

Ana Funes

Ana Funes is a 16 year old hispanic girl who still remains missing to this day.

She was last seen in Washington, Maryland on November 8th 2016. There still are no leads or suspects to the case.

Warren Lewis

Warren Lewis is a 63 year old African American man who was last seen on April 10 2017 near 1500 block of 7th Street, NW.

However,the a case filed on June 5th. He still hasn’t been found nor have many details been released about the case.
